BIO :

Konidala Pawan Kalyan (born Konidala Kalyan Babu; 2 September and he is an Indian politician and actor who has been serving as the 10th deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh since 12 June 2024. He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh. He is the founder and president of the Jana Sena Party.

As an actor, Kalyan primarily works in Telugu cinema and is known for his unique style and mannerisms. He has a huge fan base and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.

POLITICAL CAREER :

PRAJA RAJYAM PARTY

Kalyan started his political career in 2008 as the president of Yuvarajyam, the youth wing of JanaSena Party which was started by his elder brother Chiranjeevi. He neither contested in elections nor did he hold any constitutional post during his days in Praja Rajyam Party. While he actively campaigned for the party, he was also hit with few health problems. Kalyan was struck with bouts of vomiting, as he was hit by a sunstroke during a roadshow at Vizag on 19 April 2009. Later, in 2011, when Chiranjeevi merged his party with the Congress Party, Kalyan took a break from political life, expressing his silent discontent with his brother's decision to merge the party.
